We Provide

Trained Caretakers

We know how much your fur baby matters to you - they're our family! Here at Dope Pet Salon, all of our team members are Pet Sitters International (PSI) certified. We also have a good nose for good people!

Organic & Cruelty-free

We only use hypoallergenic products from kinder sources. We love ALL animals after all! Please notify us of any pet allergies and we'll make sure to accomodate in their visit.

Professional Stylists

Our groomers are personally mentored by our award-winning head stylist and animal whisperer Cassandra. Warm wash or fancy cut, they'll be handled by the gentlest hands in the industry.

Outdoor and Indoor Play

We have a large and grassy outdoor area plus a temperature-controlled indoor area so your dogs can run around no matter what the weather. We'll always have someone around to monitor and take care of shenanigans they may get into.

Cat Apartments

Your kitties will be separated by rooms complete with scratching posts and tall windows that all face the oudoor area. They can watch the birds, the dogs, or play with our team member in their regularly scheduled play time.

Special Needs

Some of our animals need a little more tender care than others. We have vet techs in our team that can provide care for a special needs animal for the day. Please contact us ahead of time to make an arrangement.
